Reuben Sandwich

Description

A Reuben sandwich is one of my family's fix-it-quick favorites. They are really
delicious and easy to make. I like to serve them with big bowls of steaming
vegetable soup and dill pickles on the side.

Ingredients

  • 8 slices rye bread
  • Â½ cup Thousand Island dressing
  • 8 slices Swiss cheese
  • 8 slices deli sliced corned beef
  • 1 cup sauerkraut, drained
  • 2 tablespoons butter, softened

Steps

  1. Step 1

    2. Preheat a large griddle or skillet over medium heat.

  2. Step 2

    3. Spread one side of bread slices evenly with Thousand Island dressing. On four
    bread slices, layer one slice Swiss cheese, 2 slices corned beef, 1/4 cup
    sauerkraut, and a second slice of Swiss cheese. Top with remaining bread
    slices, dressing-side down. Butter the top of each sandwich.

  3. Step 3

    4. Place sandwiches, butter-side down on the preheated griddle; butter the top of
    each sandwich with remaining butter. Grill until both sides are golden brown,
    about 5 minutes per side. Serve hot.
